Common AC Problems We Fix

Our team is well-equipped to address a variety of common air conditioning issues. Here are a few problems that we frequently encounter and resolve:

  1. Insufficient Cooling

    If your AC unit is running but not adequately cooling your home, it could indicate low refrigerant levels, a clogged filter, or a malfunctioning compressor. Our technicians will perform a thorough inspection to diagnose the exact problem and recommend the appropriate solution.

  2. Strange Noises

    Any unusual sounds coming from your AC, such as grinding, squealing, or banging, should not be ignored. These noises often suggest mechanical issues that require immediate attention. We can pinpoint the source of the noise and implement the necessary repairs to restore your system to normal operation.

  3. Frequent Cycling

    If your air conditioner is turning on and off repeatedly, it may be experiencing a short cycling problem. This can be caused by a variety of factors, from thermostat issues to airflow restrictions. Our team will assess the situation and ensure your unit operates efficiently.

  4. High Energy Bills

    An unexpected spike in your energy bills can indicate that your AC system is not functioning efficiently. This can be a result of various issues, including dirty filters, refrigerant leaks, or old equipment. We can help you understand the underlying problems and offer solutions to improve your system’s efficiency.

  5. Leaking Water

    A leaking air conditioning unit can be a sign of a clogged drainage line or a refrigerant leak. Both issues require prompt attention to prevent water damage to your home. We will locate the source of the leak and address it effectively.
